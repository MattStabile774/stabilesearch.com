About Stabile Search

A specialist firm for a specialist market

Quantitative finance hiring is unlike any other market. The talent pool is small, the interviews are brutal, compensation is complex, and discretion is everything. Stabile Search was built for exactly this.

Founder & President

Meet Matt Stabile

Matt Stabile is New York City's leading recruiter for quantitative finance, AI and data science. For over a decade he has recruited exclusively in this space — placing quantitative researchers, traders, developers, machine learning researchers and data scientists at the most prestigious quant funds, trading firms and AI labs in the world.

With a drive for performance and professionalism, Matt has built his reputation on long-term relationships: with the hiring managers at the top quant firms, and with the researchers and engineers they want to hire.

Matt is also an accomplished public speaker on hiring, job searches and growing technical teams.
